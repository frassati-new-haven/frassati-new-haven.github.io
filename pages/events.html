Upcoming Events

Frassati Night


Every 2nd Saturday of the month
Saturday, June 11, 2016
7:00 PM - 8:30 PM EDT, followed by a social hour

Join us for a night of prayer at St. Mary's Church (5 Hillhouse Ave, New Haven) at 7:00 p.m. The evening will include: a short talk, Eucharistic Adoration, an opportunity for the Sacrament of Reconciliation, and time to socialize in the church hall.

This month's talk “The Greatest of These: On the nature of true love,” will be offered by Br. Alan Piper, OP, who is visiting St. Mary's from the Dominican House of Studies in Washington, D.C. - please join us in welcoming Br. Alan at the social in the church hall following our time of prayer!

Frassati on Tap


Sundays, June 19, July 17, August 21
6 - 8 PM EDT

Please join us for our Summer Series on the Catholic Faith at the Stony Creek Brewery in Branford! (5 Indian Neck Ave, Branford, CT 06405).

Come enjoy a pint and listen to an engaging presentation on the Catholic Faith! On June 19th, Fr. John Paul, OP will speak about “Discerning the Little (and Big) Decisions in Life.”

Frassati Night (+ Divine Mercy!)


Saturday, April 9, 2016
7:00 PM - 8:30 PM EDT, followed by a social hour

Saturday, April 9th marks the inauguration of Frassati New Haven with a night of worship at St. Mary’s Church! Join us in adoration of the Lord in the Eucharist through a chanted Divine Mercy Chaplet and accompanied song. The evening will also include a short talk and opportunities for confession.

This month, we welcome Fr. John Paul Walker, O.P. to speak about Divine Mercy in honor of The Year of Mercy. After our time of worship, we will host a social for young adults in their 20s and 30s in the parish hall. We hope you can join us!
