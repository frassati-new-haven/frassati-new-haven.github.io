Upcoming Events

Frassati Night


Every 2nd Saturday of the month
Saturday, July 9, 2016
7:00 PM - 8:30 PM EDT, followed by a social hour

Join us for a night of prayer at St. Mary's Church (5 Hillhouse Ave, New Haven) at 7:00 p.m. The evening will include: a short talk, Eucharistic Adoration, an opportunity for the Sacrament of Reconciliation, and time to socialize in the church hall.

This month's talk is on True Freedom in Christ. Please join us for a social in the church hall following our time of prayer!
