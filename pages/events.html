Upcoming Events

Frassati Night


Every 2nd Saturday of the month
Saturday, September 10 2016
7:00 PM - 8:30 PM EDT, followed by a social hour

Come join us on September 10th for a night of prayer at St. Mary Church in New Haven! (5 Hillhouse Ave). The evening will include time for worship in Adoration, a short reflection, and the opportunity for the sacrament of Reconciliation.

This month Fr. Jonathan Kalisch, OP will offer a reflection about St. Teresa of Calcutta in honor of her canonization on September 4th! Please join us for a social in the church hall following our time of prayer!

East Rock Hike


Thursday, September 15
5:30 - 7 PM EDT

Please join us for a weekday sunset hike in East Rock park. We will meet at 5:30pm under the College Woods Pavilion at East Rock Park and hike to the top for sunset. This will be a casual hike.

Frassati on Tap


Sundays, June 19, July 17, August 21, September 18, and October 16
6 - 8 PM EDT

Please join us for our Summer Series on the Catholic Faith at the Stony Creek Brewery in Branford! (5 Indian Neck Ave, Branford, CT 06405). Come enjoy a pint and listen to an engaging presentation on the Catholic Faith!

Ben Fehrman-Lee, our worship leader at Frassati Nights, will give a talk entitled “A Theology of Hope – God of the Present Tense”, sharing some of his life’s experiences in relationship to what Christ describes as the Kingdom of God among us.
